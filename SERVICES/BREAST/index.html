Breast Cancer:

Breast cancer is is a complex disease and a type of malignancy that originates in the cells of the breast.

Early detection plays a crucial role in facilitating effective treatment and increasing the chances of a positive outcome.

These are some tips for preventing Breast Cancer:

1- Regular Self-Exams: Conduct monthly breast self-examinations to detect any unusual changes.
2- Clinical Screenings: Schedule regular clinical breast examinations and mammograms, especially if you are in a high-risk category.
3- Maintain a Healthy Lifestyle: Adopt a healthy diet, engage in regular physical activity, and limit alcohol intake.
4- Know Your Family History: Be aware of your family's breast cancer history and discuss it with your healthcare provider.
