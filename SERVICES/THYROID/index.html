Thyroid Cancer:

Thyroid cancer affects the thyroid gland, a small butterfly-shaped organ located in the neck. This cancer arises when abnormal cells in the thyroid grow uncontrollably, forming a tumor. Early detection is crucial for effective treatment and positive outcomes.

There are some tips for preventing Thyroid Cancer:

1- Regular Self-Exams: Perform regular neck self-examinations to detect any unusual lumps or changes.
2- Iodine Intake: Ensure a diet with sufficient iodine, a vital nutrient for thyroid health.
3- Limit Radiation Exposure: Minimize exposure to radiation, especially in the head and neck region.
4- Regular Check-ups: Attend routine medical check-ups, and consult your healthcare provider for any thyroid-related concerns.
